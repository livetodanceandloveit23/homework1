    About Me

    As a data analyst with a graphic design background, I have the unique ability to present numbers-based information in a way that is highly visual, easy to follow and provides answers to some of the most critical questions. I strive to make complex information visually understandable for end-users of all levels of expertise. I enjoy working hard to solve problems. I am self-motivated but believe that collaboration is vital to success and creates an environment for continuous learning and growth. My time management, meticulous organization, and collaborative best practices led to promotions, added abilities and dedication and over eight years of experience in the healthcare, consulting, not-for-profit and marketing fields.
